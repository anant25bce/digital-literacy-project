Part B – Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:
 Dos

* You should adjust your privacy settings. This will help limit who sees your posts and personal info.

* Always think before you post. Consider that future employers or professors might view it.

* Use social media to connect with people. For example you can connect with alumni on LinkedIn.

* If you see bullying, harassment or suspicious accounts report them away.

* Perform your due diligence. Cross-refer everything for authenticity before sharing rumors to spread like wildfire.

Don'ts

* Don't put details online. This includes your home address, phone number or exact location.

* Avoid posting during work hours, exams, or classes. It demonstrates your poor time management skills.

* Steer clear of heated discussions and arguments. They might damage your reputation.

* When accepting friend requests from strangers, exercise caution. First, confirm their identity.

* Don't download files or Click links, from unknown sources. You might get malware.
